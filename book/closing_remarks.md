# Closing Remarks 

Thank you for participating in this mini-module, Planning for RDM! We aimed to provide information, examples, and practice opportunities so that you could develop core competencies and knowledge necessary to plan for research data management during your master's thesis project. 

We focused on these enduring understandings and skills: 

- Understanding why research data management is important and beneficial 

- Giving detailed description of the type(s) of research data that will be used for your project. 

- Identifying whether your project will involve data that flag special ethical and/or legal considerations.  

- Creating a plan for detailed data documentation and metadata. 

- Creating a plan to store and share research data as openly as possible. 

- Planning for data management and making research data FAIR (findable, accessible, interoperable, re-usable). 

- Explaining the life cycle of data to be used for your project. 

We wish you the best with your master's thesis project! We would love to hear from you how this mini-module shaped your thesis planning process.
